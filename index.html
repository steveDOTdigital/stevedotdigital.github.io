This page says hello to Russian websites, A LOT (please share with friends)!
It is best used with a VPN to avoid being blocked.

How it works/what it does:
- it randomly openes (in a new tab) a page from the listed pages in the right-hand column
- it shows when and how often a page was opened in the current 10 minute segment
- you can modify the list of pages as you please (the list is editable)
- it does that over and over again ever 10 seconds
- it checks for list updates every 10 minutes
- it anonymises that the request came from this website
- using a VPN will anonymise your own IP address
- it performs a "stress test" on the sites listed (especially if millions use it)
- if one of the tested sites slows down or becomes unavailable the stress was too high
- it works on desktop + mobile and also measures how many "tests" were performed
